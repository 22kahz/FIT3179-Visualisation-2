CO₂ Emissions Around the World 2023

Small, wealthy countries like Qatar have the highest per capita emissions whereas large countries such as China are the top contributors to total emissions but their per capita emissions vary.

Developed countries like Australia have high per capita emissions.

Sector Emissions of the Top 10 Emitting Countries

The top 10 per capita emitters are mostly small, wealthy countries or developed countries.

The electricity and heat sector is typically the largest contributor to per capita CO₂ emissions, followed by transport and industry.

Australia, the US, and Canada also have high per capita emissions, with significant contributions from transport and industry, due to large land areas, high car ownership, and resource-based industries. In some cases, Land-use change and forestry emissions are negative, showing the benefits of reforestation.
